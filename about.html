Our Story

In the bustling heart of Kampala, where the vibrant energy of Uganda pulsed through every street, a small but ambitious computer company named Teetech was born. Its founder, a brilliant and determined young woman named Stacy, had a clear vision for the future. This led to Teetech's comprehensive mission statement: to design, develop, and distribute user-friendly, robust, and cost-effective computer hardware and software, accompanied by exceptional customer service and support, tailored to the unique needs of the Ugandan people.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, scalable, and user-friendly technology solutions that empower our clients to achieve their business objectives.
